# Was That You?

Hey, oh was that you on the street today\
Or were you that girl I left crying yesterday\
\
Hey, were you just leaving or coming back to stay\
Or were you just having coffee, oh are you ok\
\
Did you finally find the words you wanted to say\
Or was it just a dream that slipped away\
Are you still watching shadows, being played\
Reading the news, every day\
\
Hey, oh was that you on the street today\
Or was it a ghost that came my way\
Have you found Jesus, learned to pray
