# You The Right

How can you write a perfect song\
When everyone around you\
Is getting it all so wrong\
\
How can you dream, of what it ought to be\
When everything that’s beautiful\
Soon be wash to sea\
\
Maybe Now’s the time to leave\
Aren’t you coming?\
\
What gives you the right to call me a fool?\
For playing hooky from school\
That’s manifesting itself now\
As a failing state so cruel\
\
How can you stay, In front of screens that play\
Endless cycles on repeat\
Assassinations and prayers\
\
But I got family here\
The coastline so near\
A hairdresser that calls me dear\
Gunshots I don’t fear\
And the sirens they’re so far a part\
And my car, it rarely doesn’t start\
And I’m as calm as a Tibetan monk\
Don’t you know, I just block it out\
\
But how can you write, anything at al\
When everything accepted here\
To be both right and wrong\
\
How can you stay, with me another year\
Expecting I’ll get better at\
Keeping one leg here
