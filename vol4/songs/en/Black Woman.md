# Black Woman

Black man walking by the side of the road\
Where he’s heading no one knows\
Must be a stranger in this town\
Got nobody to drive him around\
\
‘Cause he’s a black man\
\
Black woman standing on the boarding line\
Gets picked up for baggage fine\
Says she’s flown fine yesterday\
But todays is just another day\
\
'Cause she’s a black woman\
\
Airline agent say I look like Pacino\
Must be my age or my undone hair-do\
Guess I’m carrying my guitar\
Must be taking me for a star\
\
'Cause I’m a white man\
\
But on the trail I got no visa\
Solo mountains for my vista\
Morning coffee, friendly barista\
No one cares about my casta\
Prenotare ho comprato una casa\
Local girl, her name was Mara\
Planted eggplants nel il sua terra\
Bring back a shirt for Cara Chiara\
\
Black woman now boarded the plane\
Got no baggage, must have paid\
No escaping the sizing cage\
Guess she’s used to turning the page\
\
'Cause she’s a black woman
